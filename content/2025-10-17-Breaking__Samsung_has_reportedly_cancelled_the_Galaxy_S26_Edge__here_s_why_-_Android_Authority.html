In a surprising development, Android Authority reports that Samsung has reportedly scrapped plans for the highly anticipated Galaxy S26 Edge. This significant move, if confirmed, signals a major shift in Samsung's flagship smartphone strategy.

According to the report, the cancellation stems from various factors, potentially including a desire to streamline its premium lineup and avoid cannibalizing sales of the popular Ultra model. Consumers eagerly awaiting the next generation of Samsung flagships will now likely see a more concentrated effort on other Galaxy S26 variants, possibly highlighting the standard and Ultra models. This strategic realignment aims to optimize resources and enhance the focus on its top-tier offerings.
