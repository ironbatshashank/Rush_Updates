NBC News: Pope Leo XIV Playfully Trolls Chicago Cubs from Vatican City

In an unexpected moment reported by NBC News, Pope Leo XIV brought humor to Vatican City by playfully "trolling" the Chicago Cubs during his weekly general audience.

Addressing pilgrims, the Pontiff quipped, "While faith can move mountains, even I, as Pope, sometimes wonder if it can move a World Series trophy to Wrigley Field every single year." The remark, met with widespread laughter, quickly went viral.

This good-natured jab delighted baseball fans and those who appreciate the Pope's lighthearted side. The papal wit showcasing a unique blend of religious authority and America's favorite pastime is now a global talking point, cementing Pope Leo XIV's status as a modern, engaging leader.
