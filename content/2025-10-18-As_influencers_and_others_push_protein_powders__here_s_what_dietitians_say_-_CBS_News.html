Protein Powder Hype: What Dietitians Really Say Amid Influencer Push (CBS News)

As social media influencers increasingly champion protein powders for muscle gain and weight loss, many consumers wonder about their true health benefits. A recent CBS News report dives deep, revealing what registered dietitians truly advise.

Experts emphasize a food-first approach, noting most individuals can meet their daily protein intake through a balanced diet of lean meats, dairy, legumes, and nuts. While protein supplements can benefit certain groups like elite athletes or those with specific dietary needs, they aren't essential for everyone. Dietitians caution against excessive reliance, highlighting the importance of ingredient quality and potential for overconsumption. For personalized nutrition advice, consulting a dietitian is key to optimizing your protein intake safely and effectively.
