John Bolton Indicted: Read Full Charges Against Ex-Trump Adviser on PBS

Former National Security Adviser John Bolton, a prominent critic of ex-President Donald Trump, has been indicted. PBS reports the U.S. Justice Department has filed charges against Bolton, alleging he illegally disclosed classified information in his forthcoming memoir without proper government clearance.

This high-profile legal action intensifies the ongoing debate over national security and freedom of speech, especially concerning former high-ranking officials. The indictment against the former Trump aide highlights a significant legal battle. For complete details and to read the full indictment documents, visit the official PBS News website.
