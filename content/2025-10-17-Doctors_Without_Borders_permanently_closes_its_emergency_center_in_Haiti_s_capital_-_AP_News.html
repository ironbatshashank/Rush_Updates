Doctors Without Borders (MSF) Permanently Closes Emergency Center in Haiti's Capital

AP News reports Doctors Without Borders (MSF) has permanently closed its crucial emergency center in Port-au-Prince. Citing extreme violence and insecurity, the humanitarian group's withdrawal leaves a devastating void in urgent medical care for Haiti's vulnerable population. This highlights the deepening crisis and the severe challenges facing essential services amidst the escalating instability.
