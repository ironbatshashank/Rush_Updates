Mariners Lifeless in ALCS Home Losses: Can Seattle Turn It Around?

The Seattle Mariners have stumbled dramatically in the ALCS, looking surprisingly lifeless during their recent home losses. Fans at T-Mobile Park witnessed a struggling offense and a team unable to generate the spark needed to compete.

Now facing a significant deficit, the question looms large: Can this Mariners squad, known for its grit and comeback potential, rediscover their form? The pressure is immense as they look to turn around their ALCS fortunes and keep their World Series dreams alive. The Seattle Times ponders their path forward, hoping for a defiant comeback.
